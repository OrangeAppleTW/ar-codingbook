Playful Albert's Space Adventures

storybooks for kids to learn coding through AR

Hello, we are a team of enthusiasts for coding education. We believe everyone should know a little bit about coding, so we are developing an AR storybook that teaches kids how to code!

Why Kids Need to Know About Coding

Coding improves problem-solving skills, logical thinking and creativity.

Our Motives

While there are a variety of coding lessons, educational robots and developing sandboxes around in the current market, kids still lack motivation as to why they should code, or what they can code for. More often than not, coding tasks such as “printing out numbers from 1 to 10” simply make coding education for kids unappealing to them. Therefore, how to engage kids in learning becomes our main concern.

Our Objectives

1. To increase kids’ motivation for coding through stories

Kids might not be interested in practical problems, but they surely love games and stories! The Playful Albert series features amusing characters, meaningful stories and exquisite illustrations. We hope the story will intrigue the kids and inspire them to help the characters overcome obstacles with their coding skills.

2. To teach kids coding in a fun and easy way

We designed an easy-to-learn, easy-to-use coding system, where kids can start coding without getting intimidated by tedious instructions. Any kids over 5 will be able to create a program within 5 minutes and make our main character Albert move around! Furthermore, with our AR app, Albert will hop onto the book, and follow the kids’ instructions to carry out tasks, making the coding experience even more fun!
